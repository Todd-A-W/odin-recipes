Egg and Spinach Breakfast Sandwich

Finding time to cook breakfast can be difficult, but these delicious egg and spinach sandwiches will help make your morning quick and nutritious!

Ingredients

  • Large Eggs. (2 Eggs, or 100g)
  • English Muffin.
  • Baby Spinach. (20g)
  • Cheddar Cheese. (30g)
  • Salt.
  • Pepper.

Steps

  1. Chop spinach and set aside.
  2. Crack eggs into bowl and whisk. (A fork is fine to whisk with, we aren't too fancy for bruised eggs)
  3. Add spinach to eggs.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Gently whisk one final time.
  6. Set a pan on medium-high heat.
  7. Put English muffin in toaster.
  8. Add eggs to pan, stirring gently until eggs reach desired consistency.
  9. Reduce heat and add a slice of cheese on top of eggs
  10. Remove pan from burner and place eggs on english muffin.
