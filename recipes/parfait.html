High Protein Yogurt Parfait!

This is a fast and easy staple food for me that is amazing when you're prepping for the week, or as a convenient and relatively healthy snack. Regardless of how you choose to use this recipe it's a fantastic way to get in some Protein and a much needed serving of fruit. If you're aiming for an even higher protein meal simply swap the yogurt and granola out for one of the many high protein versions available!

Ingredients

  • Yogurt of your choosing. (175g)
  • Frozen or fresh berries. (120g)
  • Granola mix. (50g)

Steps

  1. In a mason jar or sealable container add 175g of yogurt.
  2. Add 120g of berries. If using frozen berries it is best to thaw and drain them before adding to the yogurt to avoid excess water.
  3. Finally add 50g of your granola mix.
