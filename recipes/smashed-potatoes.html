Air Fryer Smashed Potatoes

Another fast and convenient staple food for me are air fryer smashed potatoes, they require minimal ingredients, minimal time, and most of all tastes incredible!

Ingredients

  • Baby potatoes.
  • Olive oil.
  • Grated parmesan.
  • Salt.
  • Onion powder.
  • Garlic powder.
  • Salt.

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Salt water and boil potatoes until tender. (About 15 minutes)
  3. Lay potatoes out on a sheet tray and let cool for 10 minutes.
  4. Using a glass press down on potatoes with enough pressure to flatten them, but still leave them intact.
  5. Transer potatoes to air fryer.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and add seasonings to taste.
  7. Air fry the potatoes for 7-10 minutes at 390 degrees fahrenheit.
